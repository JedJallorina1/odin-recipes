Buttermilk Fried Chicken

Description

This recipe describes how to make Southern fried chicken Texas style. I grew up eating this stuff, not very healthy but very very delicious!

My momma used to make me this cornbread when I was a kid.

Ingredients

  • Cornmeal
  • Corn starch
  • Flour
  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk (you can also use vinegar and milk. The rest of this recipe is a joke but this is an actual thing believe it or not.)
  • Eggs
  • Some delicious sides, like Southern style Mac, Collards, Candied Yams, etc.

Directions

  1. Mix the wet ingredients.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in another bowl.
  3. Mix all the ingredients together.
  4. Fry the chicken in hot oil, serve with the delicious sides!
